About Boss'Boss

Boss'Boss is a helpful scheduling tool for LaTech students, which tries to be a better interface for B.O.S.S.

It features a search bar with filters for finding interesting classes, along with a weekly schedule preview so you can see how your week looks.

You can also connect Facebook to sync your schedule accross devices and compare your schedule with Facebook friends.

It was also not made circa 1990.

The code is available on GitHub.
