Boss'Boss

is a Course Discovery and Schedule Creation tool for LA Tech Students

It has some nifty features that make it better than the existing B.O.S.S. website.

  1. Search bar for course discovery
    • CourseCode ('ENGR-121')
    • Course Number ('121')
    • Course Name ('Statics')
    • Course Description ('cultural')
    • Teacher ('Swanbom')
    • Subject ('ENGR', 'biomed')
    • Call Number ('31531')
  2. No Session Expiry
    • In fact, your session is saved automatically. Come back later and your schedule will still be here.
    • Like, seriously. If WWIII happens in the middle of your schedule creation, as long as your computer survives, you'll be able to finish your schedule.
  3. Helps you plan your schedule
    • Boss'Boss even gives you a calendar display so you can visualize your week.
    • GoogleCalendat/iCal exporting coming in the future when I have time.
  4. Was not made circa 1990
